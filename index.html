Electrical EPC & Maintenance

We undertake complete electrical EPC & maintenance of industrial & plant facilities:

  • MV/LV cables underground and overhead networks
  • Control & Fiber Optic Cable systems
  • Industrial & plant lighting, road/street lighting, building lightings
  • MV/LV sub-stations, power/distribution transformers, motors, switchgears, RMU, MCCS, panels
  • Overhauling and rehabilitation of all kinds of electrical equipment
  • Cable management system
  • Lightning protection and earthing system
  • Fire alarm, CCTV, audio visual, public address, security and access control system
  • Telecommunication and management system
  • Industrial battery banks, charger unit and UPS systems

Scaffolding Services

  • Exterior walls — multiple trades
  • System scaffolding
  • Frame and brace scaffolding
  • Tube and clamp scaffolding
  • Suspended platforms
  • High rises — swing stage
  • Scaffold labor for installation & removal

About FSC

Falah Saud Alyami Co. (FSC) is a trusted Saudi-owned contracting establishment and well-reputed service provider operating across industrial, construction, civil, and commercial sectors in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

With deep experience in construction manpower deployment and certified industrial operations, we help leading developers and contractors execute projects efficiently, legally, and on schedule.
